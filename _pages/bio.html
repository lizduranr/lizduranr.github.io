Liz Durán Rodríguez

I start with this art in 2008, studying photography in "Taller 5 design center" (Bogotá, Colombia) knowing genres such as advertising photography, fashion photography, documentary photography, artistic photography and photojournalism.

In 2011 begin to share my passion for photography, I work for Caracol TV, in the magazine of the reality show "My Name IS" first season.

In 2012 I worked on a project "Corazón Pacifico" with Oxfam ong to teach photography to indigenous communities like the Embera of Chigorodó, the Awa of Pasto and Afro communities in Chocó, so that they learn to show people their costrumbres, culture and how they live in their communities. With photos of them a calendar for 2013 was performed.

In September 2013 I work to the news paper "El Espectador" where I know the best photojournalists of Colombia, and a place which i have learned many more things about photography, and I owe much more experience in my career.

In 2015 i work as a freelance photographer, working for a production company called "Audiovisual District" doing various jobs for the ministry of technology (MINTIC) for CHEVROLET, CENCOSUD and other companies.
